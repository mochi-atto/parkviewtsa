Technology Day

When?

October 10th (last day of fall break)

Where?

Tech Day is held in Perry, GA at the Georgia National Fairgrounds & Agriculture Center.

Cost?

The cost for this trip is typically $60 on MyPaymentsPlus, which will be active between August 23rd and September 3rd.

What is it?

Tech Day is an opportunity to celebrate technology through several competitive events. Some competitions will be pre-judged while others will be judged on-site. Activities, games, and shops are also availible at the fairgrounds.

How do I go?

Seats will be allocated based on activity levels in TSA and participation in the competitions for this event. Google forms will be provided during general meetings to apply.

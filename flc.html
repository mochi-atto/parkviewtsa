Fall Leadership Conference

When?

November 11th - November 13th

Where?

FLC is held in Jekyll Island, GA at the Jekyll Island Convention Center.

Cost?

This trip typically costs $265 on MyPaymentsPlus, which will be open from September 15th to October 7th.

What is it?

FLC provides Georgia TSA members and advisors the opportunity to increase their leadership, communication, and teambuilding skills by attending hands-on workshops, seminars and general sessions. This conference also provides for chapter members and advisors to gain knowledge and skills in relation to our Competitive events Program by attending hands on workshops, seminars, and sessions during our Technical Sessions in preparation for the State Leadership Conference.

How do I go?

Seats allocation will be determined activity levels in TSA and participation in the State Leadership Conference events.

Community Service

Stem Academy

This school year, Parkview TSA hosted a STEM Academy in collaboration with Camp Creek Elementary School. This provided participants with the opportunity to create hands-on STEM projects, such as simple robots and hydraulics. Additionally, we are helping to foster an interest in STEM at an early age!

Adopt-A-Road

This year Parkview TSA is continuing our service to our community by participating in Adopt-A-Road. Parkview TSA has a mile long stretch of road, where we are responsible for picking up litter. Every other month, we organize student teams to go out and pick up trash along the side of the road. This is a great way for people to get involved in the local community and make a positive impact on their surroundings.

Community Service

Parkview TSA participated in various community service activities throughout the year, including a Thanksgiving Canned Food Drive for the Lilburn Co-Op and a Christmas Toys For Tots Donation Drive for the Salvation Army.

School Supply Drive

In partnership with the Caleb Foundation, Parkview TSA hosted a School Supply Drive, raising vast amount of school supplies such as: pens, pencils, book bags, binders, etc. that were all donated to Ghana, Africa.

Canned Food Drive

In partnership with Parkview DECA, we collaborated on a Canned Food Drive donation where the proceeds with support the Lilburn Co-Op.

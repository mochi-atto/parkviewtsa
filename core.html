Chapter Officer Retreat of Excellence

Officers Only

When?

September 10th - September 12th

Where?

CORE is held in Clayton, GA at the Kaplan Mitchell Retreat and Conference Center.

Cost?

The cost for this trip is typically $150 on MyPaymentsPlus, which will be open in August (specific date TBD).

What is it?

The Chapter Officer Retreat of Excellence (CORE) program is designed to provide Georgia TSA local chapter officers with an opportunity to develop interpersonal and leadership skills, gain necessary knowledge and abilities to carry out the duties of their individual offices, and prepare a Chapter Program of Work for the upcoming year.

How do I go?

In order to attend this trip, you must be a Parkview TSA officer and be approved to attend by Ms. Calvert.
